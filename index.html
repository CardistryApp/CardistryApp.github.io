Cards Against Singularity

Long Story Short

So in case you were wondering what this is all about, here's the skinny:

  • Single Player Cards Against Humanity
  • Two Modes of Play: Player or Czar
  • Hilarious Card Combinations
  • Submit your favorite combos to be judged
  • Immediately feel terrible about laughing
  • Others find it funny? Get an awesome point!
  • Do Your Part: Judge your friends AND strangers!
  • Shut up, Let's play
